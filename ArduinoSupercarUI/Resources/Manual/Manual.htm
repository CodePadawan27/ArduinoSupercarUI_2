Arduino Supercar Official Help


Welcome to the official Arduino Supercar help. Our team is very happy that you have chosen Arduino Supercar product
and we hope that you will have happy moments with it.


This is the main screen of the Arduino Supercar UI.


From the File menu you can close the application and from the Information menu
you can open this help file and check out the Arduino Supercar team members.


Connection can be established from the application to the RC-car by inserting
RC-car's IP-address and port number into the Connection textboxes and pressing
Connect button. To disconnect press the Connect button again.


RC-car can be controlled from the keyboard by pressing arrow keys. Brakes of the car are controlled from the keyboard's SPACE-key.
and car lights can be turned on and off with "L"-key from the keyboard.


To start the webcam stream, select your USB-connected or internal webcam
from the dropdown menu and press Start button. To stop the webcam stream press Stop button.
Still pictures also can be taken by pressing Take a picture button


After pressing Take a picture button you can choose the folder where to save the picture.

For a larger view of the webcam screen, press the Popout-button
